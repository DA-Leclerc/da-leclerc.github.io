## From Avionics to Algorithms

After 21 years as a Warrant Officer in the Canadian Armed Forces — specializing in avionics maintenance, quality auditing under AF9000+, and running the instruction cell — I made a deliberate pivot into AI governance and consulting.

It wasn't a random career change. The same skills that kept aircraft systems safe and compliant — systematic auditing, process discipline, risk management, and training others to follow frameworks — are exactly what organizations need as they adopt AI.

My approach is grounded in what works. I've spent two decades in environments where quality systems aren't optional — they're what keep people safe. I bring that same rigor to AI adoption, governance, and compliance.

I specialize in helping organizations understand where they stand with AI (through structured maturity assessments), what regulations apply to them (Loi 25, AIDA, EU AI Act), and how to build internal processes that make AI adoption sustainable — not just a one-off pilot that never scales.

## Based in Saguenay, Working Across Quebec and Canada

I'm based in Saguenay, QC, and work with organizations across Quebec and Canada. All services are available in both French and English.

Whether it's a half-day workshop for your leadership team, a full AI readiness assessment, or ongoing advisory work — I'm here to help you get AI right.
