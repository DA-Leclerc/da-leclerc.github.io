## My Approach

Every engagement starts with understanding your context. I'm not pushing a pre-built solution.

Good AI governance is:

**Practical** — Frameworks your team can actually follow. Not shelfware.

**Proportionate** — Controls matched to your risk level. Not one-size-fits-all.

**Progressive** — Designed to evolve as your AI maturity and regulations evolve.
