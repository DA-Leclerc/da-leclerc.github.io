## AI Readiness Assessment

My signature offering. A structured diagnostic using a 6-Domain AI Readiness Framework covering Leadership, Data, Skills, Governance, Processes, and Innovation. Available in three editions: one for SMB leadership, one for IT leaders, and one focused on compliance.

Each domain is scored against maturity criteria. You get a personalized report with a prioritized action plan: what to fix first, what can wait, and what you're already doing right.

The assessment is free. It's how I build trust and how you find out if you actually need help.

**Best for:** Any organization considering AI adoption and wanting a clear picture of where they stand.

## ISO 42001 Readiness & AI Governance Advisory

I help you build governance frameworks aligned with ISO/IEC 42001 (the international standard for AI management systems), Quebec's Loi 25, federal PIPEDA requirements, and the EU AI Act. The goal is frameworks that are practical and proportionate, designed for your size, your sector, and your risk profile.

I'm pursuing ISO/IEC 42001 Lead Implementer and Lead Auditor certifications in 2026, bringing formal AI management system auditing capability to this work.

If your organization handles personal data, operates in a regulated sector, or is preparing for upcoming AI legislation, this is where to start.

## ESSOR Grant Navigation

I help Quebec organizations access up to $70,000 in government funding for their digital transformation through the ESSOR program, then I deliver the work.

Three phases: grant application navigation (free), diagnostic engagement (Volet 1B, up to $20K reimbursed), and implementation (Volet 1C, up to $50K reimbursed). This can be stacked with Promotion Saguenay funding for additional support.

No other AI governance consultant in the Saguenay region offers this complete package: grant access plus delivery.
