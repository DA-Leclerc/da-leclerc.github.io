## How I Work

I don't sell AI hype. I help organizations figure out where they actually stand, what regulations apply to them, and how to build internal processes that make AI work long-term. Every engagement starts with understanding your context — not pushing a pre-built solution.

---

<div class="service-card">

### AI Readiness Assessment

A structured evaluation across six domains — Leadership, Data, Skills, Governance, Processes, and Innovation — that gives you a clear picture of your organization's AI maturity. You get a detailed report with a prioritized action plan, not a vague slide deck.

**Best for:** Organizations considering AI adoption but unsure where to start.

</div>

<div class="service-card">

### AI Governance & Compliance Advisory

Working through Loi 25, the proposed AIDA, and alignment with the EU AI Act. I help you build governance frameworks that are practical and proportionate — designed for your size, your sector, and your risk profile.

**Best for:** Organizations handling personal data, regulated industries, or those preparing for upcoming AI legislation.

</div>

<div class="service-card">

### Workshops & Training

Intimate group sessions (max 4 participants) designed to build real understanding — not just awareness. Topics range from AI fundamentals for leadership teams to hands-on governance framework building.

**Best for:** Leadership teams, compliance officers, and project managers who need to make informed AI decisions.

</div>

<div class="service-card">

### Private Advisory Sessions

One-on-one consulting for founders, executives, or team leads who need focused guidance on a specific AI challenge. These are high-impact, confidential sessions tailored to your exact situation.

**Best for:** Executives with specific AI strategy or compliance questions.

</div>
